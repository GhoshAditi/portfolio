# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, potential clients, and developers visiting the portfolio. They are looking for technical competence, creative flair, and professional reliability.

## Product Purpose
A premium portfolio showcase for Aditi that demonstrates technical and creative excellence. The site serves as a live demonstration of Next.js, TypeScript, and high-end Framer Motion proficiency.

## Brand Personality
Modern, Refined, Kinetic. The brand is built on a foundation of clean, contemporary editorial design with a focus on intentional, high-performance motion (Framer-style).

## Anti-references
- Glassmorphism overload (avoid the "frosted glass" cliché)
- Generic SaaS landing page templates
- Jarring, slow, or purely decorative animations
- The old teal and orange color palette

## Design Principles
- **Kinetic Precision**: Every motion must feel snappy and intentional, serving the content rather than distracting from it.
- **Editorial Cleanliness**: Use "Mozilla Text" and ample white space to create a modern, high-end publication feel.
- **High-Contrast Impact**: Leverage the bold red (#FF1313) against near-black (#020605) to create a striking visual identity.
- **Snappy Utility**: Prioritize performance and immediate feedback in all interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast compliance for the primary red/black/white combinations.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
- Semantic HTML and ARIA standards for assistive technology.
